Company Profile

Bangalore's Premier
Technology-Driven Transport

Founded in September 2005, White Tiger Technology Drivers Pvt. Ltd. began as a small outsourcing transport service catering to the booming IT sector in Bangalore. Today we operate a fleet of over 120 vehicles — from economy hatchbacks to executive coaches — serving individuals, corporations, and government bodies across Karnataka.

Our name reflects our ambition: the white tiger is a rare creature — bold, precise, and unstoppable. That is how we approach every ride: with discipline, professionalism, and a commitment to getting you there safely and on time.
